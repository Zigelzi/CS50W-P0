EV terminology

Here is the basic terms and acronyms related to electric vehicles.

Term Explanation
BEV Battery Electric Vehicle - Vehicle which is propelled completely with batteries. Does not have Internal Combustion Engine at all.
ICE Internal Combustion Engine - The "original" engine which is used to propel vehicles. Heat engine which uses diesel, gasoline or other kind of fuel to create motion in vehicles.
